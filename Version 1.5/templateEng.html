You can use CTRL + F or CMD + F for a quick search.
    IVRR shortens your time working through recordings from webinars
    and presentation records.

    How does this work?
    In the left window you can see the webinar or the video recording of a presentation.
    In the right window you can see keywords that IVRR was able to find in the recording.

    Now use the Ctrl + F to search for keywords that interest you.
    With a double click on the keyword, the video automatically jumps to the corresponding point in the recording.
    Of course, you can also simply scroll through the key words like bullet points and find the ones that interest you,
    then Double-click these words.
    Listen to only those sequences that are relevant to you and come faster
    to the point.

    Good luck with IVRR
